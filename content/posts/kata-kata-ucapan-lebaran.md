<p>Lebaran is one of the most celebrated occasions in Indonesia. It is a time when families and friends gather to celebrate the end of the month-long fasting period of Ramadan. During this time, Muslims all over the world exchange greetings and well wishes to one another. Here are some Kata Kata Ucapan Lebaran that you can use to wish your loved ones a happy and prosperous Lebaran:</p>

<h2>Kata Kata Ucapan Selamat Lebaran</h2>

<center><center>
	<img src="https://sijai.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Kata-Kata-Lebaran-800x450.jpg" alt="Selamat Lebaran" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
	<small>Source: <a href="https://www.bing.com" rel="nofollow">bing.com</a></small>
</center></center>

<p>1. Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum. (May Allah accept from us and from you.)</p>
<p>2. Mohon maaf lahir dan batin. (Please forgive me for any wrongdoings, both physically and spiritually.)</p>
<p>3. Salam takzim dan hormat. (Respectful greetings and salutations.)</p>
<p>4. Selamat hari raya Idul Fitri. (Happy Eid al-Fitr.)</p>
<p>5. Semoga kita dipertemukan dengan Ramadhan yang penuh berkah lagi. (May we be reunited with a blessed Ramadan again.)</p>

<h2>Kata Kata Ucapan Mohon Maaf Lahir Batin</h2>

<center><center>
	<img src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-BQIGRzZqG_Q/WxevN81MOqI/AAAAAAAADiU/odp1ajuiyHsQorspzE3EKfVeahEBvHWMQCLcBGAs/s1600/Kumpulan%2BPantu%2BLebaran.png" alt="Mohon Maaf Lahir Batin" width="800" height="500" style="display: block; width: 100%; height: auto">
	<small>Source: <a href="https://www.bing.com" rel="nofollow">bing.com</a></small>
</center></center>

<p>1. Mohon maaf lahir batin ya, semoga kita selalu diberikan keikhlasan hati. (Please forgive me both physically and spiritually, may we always be given sincerity in our hearts.)</p>
<p>2. Selamat hari raya Idul Fitri, mohon maaf lahir dan batin. (Happy Eid al-Fitr, please forgive me for any wrongdoings, both physically and spiritually.)</p>
<p>3. Mohon maaf lahir batin atas segala kesalahan yang telah saya lakukan. (Please forgive me both physically and spiritually for all the mistakes I have made.)</p>
<p>4. Mohon maaf lahir dan batin, semoga kita selalu diberikan kesehatan dan kebahagiaan. (Please forgive me both physically and spiritually, may we always be given health and happiness.)</p>
<p>5. Mohon maaf lahir batin, semoga kita selalu diberikan keberkahan dan ketentraman dalam hidup. (Please forgive me both physically and spiritually, may we always be given blessings and peace in life.)</p>
